
This is a story of a boy named Nick who lived on the outskirts of town.

He was the only one in his family, as his parents passed away when he was little. He was all alone in a house in
the woods. Nick visited the priest of the nearby church every morning. The priests name is David.He took care of 
the boy and visited him often when was free to.

One night when Nick went to bed he could hear something from outside his house........a strange noise which was 
coming from behind his house. There was a dead tree there. Nick was frightened, he did not know what to do. He did 
not have a phone to contact anyone.he could hear something at his front door, "It must be a wolf" he thought.
